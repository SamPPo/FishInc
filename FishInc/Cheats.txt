# FishInc Cheats
# Open the console with ` (backtick) during Play In Editor.

GiveCurrency Coins <Amount>
  - Adds the specified amount of Coins directly to the EconomySubsystem.
  - Example: GiveCurrency Coins 1000

GiveCurrency Fish <Amount>
  - Adds the specified amount of Fish directly to the EconomySubsystem.
  - Example: GiveCurrency Fish 500

GiveCurrency Pearls <Amount>
  - Adds the specified amount of Pearls directly to the EconomySubsystem.
  - Example: GiveCurrency Pearls 10
